Monitoring the three orangutan species

This dashboard consolidates conservation-relevant data for Pongo abelii, Pongo pygmaeus, and Pongo tapanuliensis: species range maps, near-real-time deforestation and fire alerts, literature, threats, and incident monitoring. Data refreshes automatically via scheduled jobs; the site is static and simply loads the processed files.

Phase 1 (proof of concept): the Map tab shows the Tapanuli orangutan range with Global Forest Watch integrated deforestation alerts. More species and tabs are being added, including NASA FIRMS fire alerts.
